Refund Policy

At Pixel Blast, we strive to provide high-quality courses and services to our users. However, we understand that there may be circumstances where a refund is necessary. Please read our refund policy carefully.

Eligibility for Refunds
Refunds will only be considered under the following conditions:
The user must provide satisfactory reasons for the refund request via email.
Requests must be submitted within 7 days of the purchase date.

Submission Process
To request a refund, please follow these steps:
Send an email to gouthamkumara2@gmail.com with the subject line “Refund Request.”
Include the following information in your email:
Your full name
Course title
Purchase date
Payment Receipt/Payment Confirmation
Detailed explanation of your reasons for requesting a refund

Review and Response
Once we receive your refund request, we will review it and respond within 4-5 business days. We may ask for additional information if needed to process your request.

Approval or Denial
Refunds will be granted at the discretion of Pixel Blast, based on the satisfaction of the reasons provided. If your request is approved, the refund will be processed using the original payment method within 4-5 days.

Non-Refundable Items
Please note that certain items may be non-refundable, including but not limited to:
Course materials that have been accessed or downloaded.
Fees for additional services or materials that were not part of the original course purchase.

Changes to the Refund Policy
Pixel Blast reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
